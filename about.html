About Project Review Portal

Project Review Portal is a responsive web platform designed to streamline the process of evaluating student-submitted academic or personal projects.

  • After submission, students can navigate to the feedback section to view the feedback given by the admin.
  • Feedback includes a review message, a rating (out of 5 stars), and the selection status (Selected / Not Selected).

  • The dashboard includes filtering options (by rating and gender) for easy navigation.
